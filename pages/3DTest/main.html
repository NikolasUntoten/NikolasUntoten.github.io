3D Engine

Below, you can see a 3D rendering engine that I developed from scratch (originally in java, and adapted to javascript). When I say from scratch, I mean it. No libraries (aside from jquery) were used here, and everything is being drawn using built in html canvas functions. I developed all rendering algorithms myself (mostly through a lot of though, and trial and error). The only part that is not my own original work is a function for rotating a point about another point in 3D space, because this was a readily available equation.

Use WASD to move, and arrow keys to look around.
